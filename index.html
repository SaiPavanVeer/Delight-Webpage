street food is ready to eat food or drink sold by a hawker,or vendor,in a street or other public places,such as at a market or fair.

Street food safety is essential, and yet it has been rarely studied in China. Therefore, a typical city in China was selected as the research object to assess food safety knowledge, attitudes, and street food suppliers and consumer behaviors using questionnaires based on previous studies and considering China's particular characteristics and reasonable impacts identified in previous studies, such as increased income, work experience, licenses, and locations. The food safety knowledge and attitude questionnaire confirmed with the national conditions in China.

Food safety knowledge, attitudes, and behavior of street food vendors and consumers in Handan, a third-tier city in China

Food safety has long been the subject of scholarly research, and street food is a weak link in food safety supervision. Street food not only provides convenience for many people but is also the livelihood for millions of low-income people, making a great contribution to the economy of many developing countries
